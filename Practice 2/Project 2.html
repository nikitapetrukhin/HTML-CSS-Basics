Biography

"Today, Miss Monique is among the world’s fastest-rising Progressive House DJs, and probably the genre’s most recognized female DJ in Europe.

Unlike many other popular DJs today who made their name producing rather than mixing, she rose to fame by the old school back with her unprecedented conversion of classic radio mixes into YouTube podcasts.

Year after year, Miss Monique’s unique talent of smoothly melting techno rhythms with classic trance and progressive tunes gets highly praised by an ever-growing number of electronic music aficionados. By 2020, her live sets were already gathering thousands of progressive house addicts online, with recorded streams reaching up to 2 million views. Miss Monique’s most popular set on Radio Intense channel boasts a whopping 11+ million views.

On the other side of the camera

Of course, this is a personal life: what could be more important in a young woman's life when the music stops, the spotlights turn off? In this matter, y Miss Monique for people who are not privy to her non-artistic affairs, everything is a complete mystery these days. Not found at Alesya has not a single photo on Instagram with a man, so that you can say for sure: this is him, the only one and beloved. But such a person exists, although he does not want to categorically show himself in public!

In an interview for May 2021 on the YouTube channel "DJ Lutique", the host of the conversation found out from Miss Monique's name is Artem. "The one with whom we have been working in a team for ten years, and also a loved one," it was said about the mysterious Artem in the dialogue. Lutique tried to call the man and show him to the public, but alas - failure, Artem remained behind the scenes. In the same conversation, it was mentioned that the couple has not only a common job: young people actively travel and relax together.
